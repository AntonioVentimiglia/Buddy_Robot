## Criteria definitions

- **ROS/API support:** maintained ROS 2 driver or clean protocol/API.
- **Electrical fit:** voltage/current/connectors/EMI fit the power tree.
- **Mechanical fit:** mounting, dimensions, mass, cable routing.
- **Safety controls:** enable line, fault reporting, watchdog, predictable stop behavior.
- **Documentation:** datasheets, examples, protocol docs.
- **Availability:** replacements and accessories available.
- **Cost:** part plus required accessories and spares.
